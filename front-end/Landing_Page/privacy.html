Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: March 2024

At ServiceHub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our mobile applications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for an account, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, or otherwise contact us.

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Financial Data: Payment details, bank account information for payouts (processed securely via our third-party payment gateways).
  • Verification Data: For Service Providers, government-issued ID and background check data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Facilitate the matching of Customers and Service Providers based on location.
  • Process payments, payouts, and escrow holds securely.
  • Resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems via our Arbitration system.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Information is only shared in the following situations:

Between Users: Customers and Providers will see basic contact and location information regarding each other once a job is actively booked.
During Arbitration: If a dispute is raised, chat logs, photos, and job details are shared with a designated Arbitrator to reach a resolution.
Service Providers: We share data with third-party vendors who perform services for us, such as payment processing and background checks.

4. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.
