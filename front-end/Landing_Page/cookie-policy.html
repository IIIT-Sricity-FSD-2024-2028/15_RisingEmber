Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to improve your experience.

Last Updated: March 2024

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.

2. How we use cookies

ServiceHub uses cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in securely to your Dashboard.
  • Remember your location preferences for browsing local services.
  • Analyze platform traffic so we can improve the website.

3. Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to refuse to accept cookies and to delete cookies. The methods for doing so vary from browser to browser. Please note that blocking essential cookies may negatively impact your ability to use the ServiceHub platform and dashboard securely.
