Trust & Safety

Your security is our priority. Follow these guidelines for a safe experience.

Keep Payments on Platform

Never pay a provider in cash or via external wire transfers. Using ServiceHub's escrow ensures your funds are protected and eligible for arbitration if something goes wrong.

Communicate via ServiceHub

Keep all messages and agreements within the platform. If a dispute arises, our arbitrators can only review communication that occurred on our official channels.

Verify Identity

When a provider arrives, verify their identity matches their ServiceHub profile. All our providers undergo background checks, but it's always good practice to check.

Report Suspicious Behavior

If a user asks you to bypass our safety systems or makes you feel uncomfortable, report them immediately using the flag icon on their profile.
