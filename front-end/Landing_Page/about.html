Our Mission

Bringing Trust Back to Home Services

We built ServiceHub to eliminate the friction, anxiety, and unfairness that traditionally comes with hiring independent contractors.

The ServiceHub Story

For decades, the home service industry has been plagued by a lack of trust. Customers worried about being overcharged or left with incomplete work, while service providers struggled with delayed payments and unfair disputes.

ServiceHub was founded in 2024 to fix this broken system. By introducing secure escrow payments and a dedicated, neutral arbitration team, we ensure that every job is completed fairly, transparently, and securely.
